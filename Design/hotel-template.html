Listed by Conde Nast Traveler as one of the top 10 resorts in Mexico, Dreams Cancun Resort and Spa welcomes guests into a world of Unlimited Luxury. Surounded by three sides of the shimmering Caribbean Sea, Dreams Cancun ofers an unparalleled location, a creative kids program, luxury rooms and suites and plenty of fun for the whole family.

Why We
Love It
Between the tree house, colourful swirling waterslides and waterfall buckets, the whole family will have hours of fun splashing and sliding heir way through the water park.
The mini-waterfalls in the Littl Adventure section of the water park is perfect for kids under 6 years old.
After working up an appetite, visit snack bar right beside the water park, which has fun children's menus and shaded areas perfect for cooling down. The Kid's Club, perfect for children 4 to 10 years old, offers fun and active activities such as treasure hunts, balloon wars and more!
Each Monday is Pirate Day! Kids can put on fun pirate or madmoiselle in distress costumes and enjoy special dishes in the restaurants garnished with boats, swords, flags and big hats! Activities throughout the day, such as a quest for the hidden treasure, are led by friendly pirates! Each day has a theme! The other themes are: Viva Mexico, Celebrities, Nature, Magic and Family Time. With the dinner theatre program, you can enjoy a delicious meal while watching a show with your family!
